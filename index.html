      Standard Delivery is through Royal Mail and Express Delivery through FedEx. Orders placed before 12.00 will be sent out on the same day. Any orders placed on or after 12.00 may not be dispatched until the following morning. Any orders placed on Friday after 12.00 will be dispatched Monday morning with standard next day delivery.

      To complete your return, we require a receipt or proof of purchase.

      Please do not send your purchase back to the manufacturer.

      There are certain situations where only partial refunds are granted (if applicable)

      • Book with obvious signs of use
